Recipe #2 Cake

  • Time: 10s
  • Serves: No
Ingredients Quantity
Wheat 3
Egg 1
Milk buckets 3
sugar 2

Step 1: put 3 buckets of milk on top layer of a crafting table.

Step 2: put 1 egg right in the middle of a crafting table.

Step 3: put 3 wheat on bottom layer of a crafting table.

Step 4: put 2 sugar on the midleft and midright cells of a crafting table.

Step 5: get a cake, put it on any block and take a piece.
